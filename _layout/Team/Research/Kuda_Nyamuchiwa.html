Kudakwashe Nyamuchiwa (PhD Student)

"I am a Ph.D candidate in the ADMTRL-lab, with a bachelor's degree in metallurgical engineering from the University of Zimbabwe. My passion for materials science led me to pursue a master's degree at the Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology, where I specialized in smart titanium-based alloys for biomedical applications. Currently, I am focused on the hybrid manufacturing of the newly developed alloy, M789, tailored for laser powder bed fusion. I'm enthusiastic about advancing its potential applications across industries through my research. Throughout my academic and research endeavors, I have developed expertise in alloy design and material characterization. Apart from research work I enjoy the outdoors, skiing, and guitar playing."
